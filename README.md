The explosive growth of open-source AIGC models (Stable Diffusion, ComfyUI workflows, etc.) has created a legal gray area. Creators, from hobbyists to studios, unknowingly risk copyright violations because:

*   **"Safe" models are gatekept** behind corporate walls.
*   **Transparency is lacking** in training data sources.
*   **No open-source tool exists** to audit and mitigate this risk at scale.

We are building the **missing public good** for the AIGC ecosystem.

This project will evolve through defined phases to de-risk the AIGC pipeline:

1.  **Phase 1: Compliance Scanning**

2.  **Phase 2: "Clean" Model Marketplace**

3.  **Phase 3: Attribution & Arbitration**
    *   Explore blockchain-based attribution for AI-assisted works.
    *   Provide a pathway for copyright holders and creators to resolve disputes.
